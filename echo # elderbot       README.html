Problem Statement

Due to migration for jobs and education, many elderly parents are left living alone. They often forget medicines, lack daily conversation, and feel unsafe during emergencies. Elder Bot is designed to provide care, companionship, and peace of mind.

How Elder Bot Works

Processing

Speech recognition and AI logic understand the request and make decisions.

Output

Voice replies, alerts, and messages sent to family members.
